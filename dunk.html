HOW TO PLAY?

This is a super duper complex game, so please pay attention! Basically, the first step is to roll exactly a 10 with the dice, not one over, not one under. Then after you have done that, another game will pop up. You must complete the game with an 80% accuracy. Once it is complete, you will perform and awesome dunk!

Congratulations!

You are done.


Use arrow keys to move the key to the house!
